The Peach Thing

The Peach Thing is a kind of Peach Custard-Cobbler

Description

This eggy, custardy cobbler is delightful and nourishing. Make the most of peach season and eat it for breakfast and again for dessert.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup sunflower oil
  • 1 cup white unbleached flower
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 capfli of vanilla
  • 1 pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Beat three fresh eggs in a mixing bowl. Add 1 cup turbinado sugar, Â½ to 1 cup of pure vegetable oil (recommend sunflower), a pinch of salt and a capful of vanilla extract. Mix until smooth. Dry mix: 1 cup organic white unbleached flour with 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder and a full pinch of salt. Flid dry mix into egg batter.
  3. Cut peaches into 1/8 size crescent-moon wedges. Drop into 6 by 12 pre-greased glass baking pan, toss peaches with a little sugar and oil.
  4. Pour custard mixture over peaches.
  5. Bake for approximately 20 minutes, until golden brown.
