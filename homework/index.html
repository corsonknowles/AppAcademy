        The Peach Thing (Peach Custard-Cobbler)

        Description>

        This eggy, custardy cobbler is delightful and nourishing. Make the most of peach season and eat it for breakfast and again for dessert.

        Ingredients

          3 eggs
            1 cup sugar
              1 cup sunflower oil
                1 cup white unbleached flower
                  1/2 tsp baking powder
                    1 capful of vanilla
                      1 pinch of salt

                      Directions

                        Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
                          Beat three fresh eggs in a mixing bowl. Add 1 cup turbinado sugar, Â½ to 1 cup of pure vegetable oil (recommend sunflower), a pinch of salt and a capful of vanilla extract. Mix until smooth. Dry mix: 1 cup organic white unbleached flour with 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder and a full pinch of salt. Fold dry mix into egg batter.
                            Cut peaches into 1/8 size crescent-moon wedges. Drop into 6 by 12 pre-greased glass baking pan, toss peaches with a little sugar and oil.
                              Pour custard mixture over peaches.
                                Bake for approximately 20 minutes, until golden brown.
